What We Believe

Every investment has an impact

There is no neutral. There is no "non-impact." There is only the choice of what kind of impact you are willing to own. Fiduciary duty and moral duty are complementary forces. They always were.

Think about what you do, every day, for the people you love. You invest in their health, their education, their safety, their sense of belonging. You think in generations. These are the assets that make all other assets possible. They compound invisibly, relentlessly, into futures you can already feel taking shape. We extend that same logic beyond our walls.

Asset-Based Design

We define people and communities by their strengths and aspirations rather than by deficits alone. This is more than optimism. It is a strategic orientation that produces different questions, different partnerships, and different outcomes. Solutions that build on existing strengths integrate more naturally into markets and achieve scale more readily.

Blended Value

Financial sustainability, social impact, and environmental stewardship are complementary dimensions of any meaningful value proposition. We assess every activity on financial metrics and on social and environmental impact, recognizing that value creation spans multiple dimensions simultaneously.

Design Capital

This is patient, flexible funding that gives visionary organizations the runway to test ideas, refine models, and scale what works. Design capital accepts that the most important innovations often create the conditions that make countless future returns possible, even when they cannot promise returns on traditional timelines.

Fund, Amplify, Connect

There are enough great ideas and organizations in the world. What the field needs is partners willing to find them, resource them properly, and then step back. We see ourselves as an amplifier and enabler of others' work, strengthening existing anchor institutions rather than replicating what already exists.

Our Approach

We walk across the full spectrum of capital

We provide design capital for field-building, moving fluidly from philanthropic to commercial depending on what each partnership requires. The orientation matters because it shapes every decision.

Select Few, Go Long

We deliberately partner with a small number of organizations at a time, choosing those with truly transformative potential. Real change takes time, so we commit to multi-year partnerships, providing mission/market critical capital and devoted attention through multiple cycles of growth and adaptation. These are intimate relationships that require deep mission alignment.

Tailored Capital

We structure funding to fit each partner's needs, combining instruments into a blended capital stack that matches their stage and ambition. This flexibility is essential because field-builders have diverse needs that rarely fit neatly into standard funding categories.

Trust and Respect

Organizations are selected because of their expertise and because they know more than we do. We listen and let them drive. Each team knows best what their partners and stakeholders need, so our role is to amplify their impact, provide strategic guidance, and open doors. We invest in amazing people and then go to work for our investments.

Our Principles

The commitments that guide everything we do

We direct capital close to the opportunity.

Proximity reveals what distance obscures. Context, relationships, and lived realities are how we find what others miss.

We design from strengths.

Dignity scales and deficit framing stalls. When we engage people through their assets, capabilities, and potential, we build pathways that hold.

We earn success with honor.

Discipline, integrity, and humility are how you become first among equals in the rooms that matter.

We hold trust as strategy.

Trust compounds faster than money. We say what we mean, do what we say, and invest in relationships that grow over time.

G.A.S. We give a sh*t.

Conviction and care are how we find the overlooked corners of the market where real value lives. Love and empathy are strategic advantages, and we deploy them accordingly.

We believe capital has a highest use.

That use is unlocking freedom, expanding possibility, and compounding value across markets and generations.

When frontier and emerging communities are physically, emotionally, educationally, and economically whole, the roadmap for a joyful and resilient future unfolds for us all.

This is what we are building. This is who we partner with.

The Institute

The Next Economies Institute

SRF +Impact Fund serves as the capital formation platform for the Next Economies Institute. While the Fund deploys capital, the Institute generates the research, evidence, and talent that inform where and how that capital flows.

With university partnerships spanning the United States, United Kingdom, and Switzerland, the Institute operates at the intersection of rigorous inquiry and transformative practice, testing hypotheses before capital gets deployed at scale.
