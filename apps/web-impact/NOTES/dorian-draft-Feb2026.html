Social Finance

Investing in Human Futures

We resource the organizations and leaders building the infrastructure of the next economies. Transdisciplinary research translated into practice. Patient capital deployed across global markets toward systems that serve broad prosperity.

Thesis

The infrastructure of the next economies is being built now

Accelerators are developing impact enterprises. Intermediaries are creating new markets. Policy advocates are shaping enabling environments. Universities are training the next generation of practitioners. Measurement bodies are building the standards that will define the field.

These organizations create the conditions in which capital can flow toward human futures at scale. They are the connective tissue of social finance. We find them and resource them to reach the next level.

Our role is to strengthen what exists, connect what is fragmented, and provide patient capital where it creates multiplier effects across the ecosystem.

Approach

We seek out visionary organizations and ask what they need

There are enough great ideas in the world. What the field needs is capital willing to find them, resource them properly, and then step back. We identify organizations with transformative potential and partner with them for the long term.

Design Capital

Patient, flexible funding that provides runway to test ideas, refine models, and scale what works. We walk from the philanthropic side of the capital spectrum, leading with grants and technical assistance, progressing to recoverable grants and program-related investments, employing guarantees and first-loss positions to unlock co-investment.

Select Few, Go Deep

We partner with a small number of organizations at a time, providing each with substantial capital and devoted attention. These are long-term relationships built on mission alignment and mutual commitment. We tailor funding to fit each partner's needs rather than forcing them into predetermined structures.

Fund, Amplify, Connect

We see ourselves as an amplifier and enabler of others' work. Rather than replicating what already exists, we strengthen existing anchor institutions and equip them with resources to reach the next level. Our portfolio becomes a network of organizations learning from each other.

Trust

Organizations are selected because of their expertise and proximity to the work. They understand what their communities need. Our role is to provide strategic guidance, mentorship, and access to networks. We invest in people and then go to work for our investments.

Ecosystem

Where we deploy capital

We strengthen the global infrastructure of social finance by investing in the organizations that shape the field and create multiplier effects. A well-placed investment in field infrastructure ripples out to benefit countless other efforts.

Accelerators & Venture Builders

Organizations incubating and scaling impact enterprises. A single investment in a high-performing accelerator may support dozens of ventures over time.

Market Intermediaries

Entities connecting capital to opportunity, structuring deals, and creating financial products that improve capital flows.

Policy & Advocacy

Institutions shaping the regulatory environment. A single policy change can open markets or redirect billions.

Universities & Research

Academic centers generating evidence, developing talent, and institutionalizing blended value in education.

Convenings & Communications

Platforms building community, shaping narrative, and attracting more practitioners and capital to the field.

Measurement & Standards

Organizations creating the frameworks and verification systems that build trust across the ecosystem.

The Institute

Next Economies Institute

SRF Impact serves as the advisory and capital formation platform for the Next Economies Institute, a transdisciplinary research and practice organization bridging academic inquiry with field-based implementation.

The Institute convenes scholars, practitioners, investors, and community stakeholders across geographies. It develops frameworks for human futures and next economy design, translating research into investable solutions and deploying knowledge toward underinvested communities.

Transdisciplinary Research

Applied research spanning finance, health, climate, policy, and equity. Partnering with leading universities to generate evidence and inform the design of solutions.

University-Embedded Labs

Collaborative research environments harnessing faculty expertise and student energy to prototype responses to complex challenges.

Technology Transfer

Bridging academic innovation and real-world markets. Transforming university research into ventures addressing social and environmental challenges.

Ecosystem Investments

Design capital for field-building organizations. From accelerators and policy hubs to measurement bodies and global convenings.
