Embedded Advisory

Outsourced Chief Impact Strategy Officer

For organizations seeking senior-level impact leadership, we offer an embedded advisory model that provides dedicated strategic counsel with long-term accountability. Your OCISO serves as a senior partner integrated into your leadership structure, maintaining deep institutional knowledge and continuity of perspective.

We attend board meetings, participate in executive committees, and provide the sustained engagement that impact strategy demands. This model delivers the depth and commitment of an executive hire with the breadth and independence of a trusted advisory partnership.

Core Responsibilities

The OCISO assumes responsibility across the full spectrum of impact strategy. This includes designing comprehensive strategies that align investments, philanthropy, operations, and communications into a unified framework. The OCISO facilitates alignment among family members, boards, executives, staff, and external partners around shared impact objectives, navigating complex stakeholder dynamics to build consensus and momentum.

The OCISO manages the execution of impact initiatives, coordinating with internal teams and external advisors to ensure delivery, and maintains accountability for translating strategy into action. This includes establishing impact metrics, tracking progress, and reporting outcomes to relevant stakeholders, ensuring that impact claims are grounded in evidence and that learning informs ongoing strategy refinement.

The OCISO connects clients to opportunities, partners, co-investors, grantees, and networks that advance their impact objectives, leveraging SRF Capital AG + Impact's relationships and the Next Economies Institute's intelligence on behalf of the client. The role also encompasses developing the internal capabilities of the client organization to sustain and advance impact work over time, including training staff, building systems, and cultivating next-generation leadership.

Retainer Partnership

Ongoing strategic counsel with defined commitments, regular sessions, and continuous access to your OCISO. Designed for organizations seeking sustained leadership over multi-year timeframes.

Project-Plus

Board Advisory

Who We Serve

Tailored advisory for those stewarding capital of consequence

While our core methodology is consistent, the approach is customized to the needs of each client segment.

Families and Family Offices Stewarding Capital of Consequence

Families, family offices, and individual philanthropists seeking to manage their wealth responsibly and meaningfully. These clients often have complex asset holdings and are interested in aligning their financial legacy with their values. Many are multi-generational families facing transitions or looking to engage younger members in philanthropy and social finance.

These clients gain a trusted independent advisor who ensures their wealth serves a higher purpose while remaining financially sound. We are product-agnostic and solely focused on the family's mission, helping families avoid common pitfalls and maximize the positive legacy of their wealth.

Anchor Institutions

Established organizations that hold significant capital for public or charitable benefit, including large private foundations, community foundations, university endowments, international NGOs with grantmaking arms, hospitals, and universities interested in aligning their financial assets with their social missions.

SRF Capital enables foundations and mission-driven institutions to do more with their capital, to go beyond traditional grantmaking and passive investments, and move toward a model where 100% of their assets advance their mission. We provide the strategic insight and financial acumen that busy leaders may lack in-house.

Financial Institutions & Strategic Partners

Banks, wealth managers, asset management firms, multi-family offices, and other financial intermediaries that serve high-net-worth clients. Many such institutions are looking to differentiate their services with sustainable finance or philanthropy advisory, but may lack specialized expertise internally.

By partnering with SRF Capital, financial institutions can accelerate their innovation in sustainable finance. They gain access to cutting-edge expertise, deal flow in the impact space, and a network of impact projects worldwide. This collaboration helps partners attract new client segments and meet the growing demand for purpose-driven investing.
